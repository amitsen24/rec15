About Us

Ramgarh Engineering College is a PPP based college committed to delivering top-notch education and fostering innovation. Our beautiful campus and expert faculty create the perfect environment for student success.WELCOME TO RAMGARH ENGINEERING COLLEGE An ISO 21001:2018 & ISO 9001: 2015 certified institution with NBA Accredited Programmes* Partnered with Govt. of Jharkhand. Partnered with Bosch for Skill Development. Training Centre for Youth Employability Skill Development of BOSCH India (REC-BOSCH Industry Academia Collaboration) Skill Hub for PMKVY 4.0, Govt.of India. Ramgarh Engineering College (Estd. by Govt. of Jharkhand & run by Techno India under PPP) previously known as Techno India Ramgarh and Government Engineering College, Ramgarh, established with the prior approval of All India Council For Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliation of Jharkhand University of Technology, is an institution under Public Private Partnership between the Government of Jharkhand and Techno India. It is situated in the outskirts of Ramgarh district town on NH-23 at Murubanda, near Chitarpur, Rajrappa project covering an area of 24 acres including separate hostel facilities for both boys and girls as well as staff quarters, is having a pollution free environment. The college is ISO 9001:2015 certified and TEQIP-III (World Bank and MHRD, Govt. of India) funded institute. We, have always been trying to keep our commitment towards our goal to spread education among the ‘technocrat’ of the new era without ignoring the need of proper moral development of the students of the post-modern age. We have all along tired to make our pupils realize that the hidden truth is ‘Education is the manifestation of the perfection already in man’. The college is following outcome based education(OBE) system in all of its five undergraduate (B.Tech) departments- CE, ME, CSE, ECE, and EE as prescribed by the National Board for Accreditation (NBA). Educators are trained for OBE from IIT Gadhinagar. The mentor of the college is BVBCET, Hubli (now KLETech University, one of the best engineering institute in India). Through TEQIP-III, the faculty of BVBCET and several IITs and NITs are regularly taking seminars and workshops for students. Our Faculty members are trained in several IITs and IIMs. The college is providing free GATE and Employability Skill coaching for all 3rd year and 4th Year students through TEQIP. The college frequently organizes seminars/workshop for upgradation of students where eminent resource persons are invited. Ramgarh Engineering College is going to start BCA or Bachelor of Computer Application, affiliated by JUT from the Academic year 2023-2024. * Civil Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering.
